Site Plan for Kigali Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

The site name for our chamber of commerce website is Kigali Chamber of Commerce. We chose this name because it clearly represents the city in which the chamber operates, making it easy for visitors to identify our location.

Site Purpose

Our chamber of commerce website serves two main purposes:

  1. Facilitate business networking: The website will provide information about business-to-business networking events organized by the chamber.
  2. Resource hub: It will serve as a resource hub, offering information on local businesses, economic development, community events, and policies that impact our community's economic prosperity.

Scenarios

As the target audience for a chamber of commerce website, visitors might have various questions and scenarios:

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

Our selected color scheme reflects our organization's purpose and identity:

  • Primary Color: blue to represent peace
  • Secondary Color: green to represent prosperity

Typography

We will use the following fonts for our website:

  • Heading Font: "Montserrat", sans-serif
  • Body Font: "Roboto", sans-serif
